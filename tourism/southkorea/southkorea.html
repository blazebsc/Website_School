South Korea cities, culture, and coastline

A simple guide to help you plan a trip what to see, what to eat, and a few quick tips.

Highlights

Modern cities

Seoul's neighborhoods, shopping streets, and night markets.

History & palaces

Traditional villages, temples, and royal palace grounds.

Nature

Mountain hikes, coastal walks, and scenic islands.

Top places

  • Seoul — museums, palaces, cafes, and nightlife
  • Busan — beaches, seafood, and coastal views
  • Jeju Island — volcanic landscapes and waterfalls
  • Gyeongju — historic sites and traditional culture

Must-try food

Bibimbap

Rice bowl with vegetables and sauce (often with egg/meat).

Kimchi

Fermented veggies served with many meals.

Korean BBQ

Grilled meats with side dishes and wraps.

Note: Many dishes can be spicy ask for “less spicy” if needed.

Quick travel tips

  • Use transit cards for subway/bus (fast and convenient).
  • Carry a small umbrella weather can change quickly.
  • Learn a few basics: “Hello” and “Thank you” go a long way.
Best time to visit

Spring and autumn are popular for comfortable temperatures and scenery.
